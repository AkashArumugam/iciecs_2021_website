About the Institution

R.M.K College of Engineering and Technology is promoted by Lakshmikanthammal Educational Trust, established in the year 1995 to render philanthropic and educational service to people living in rural areas bordering Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh. The main objective of the trust is to foster quality technical and higher education, research and training in various branches of Engineering and Technology. The college is affiliated to Anna University and is certified under ISO 9001:2015 by TUVNORD, accredited by NAAC with A grade and all eligible programs are accredited by NBA. Our Institution is ranked in 251- 300 band in NIRF ranking and categorized as ‘Band C’ institution in ARIIA ranking during the year 2020. Our institute has been awarded with “Platinum Category” for the best industry linked institution by AICTE-CII for the past three years.

About the Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was started in the year 2008. The department has highly qualified and dynamic faculty members specialized in advanced areas. The department has good infrastructure and it imparts high quality education structured to meet the present day needs. The department has extensive interactions with Professional bodies IEEE, ISTE, IET and IEI. The department has established 6 Centres of Excellence and signed 11 MOUs for industry linkage. The department is regularly organizing Conferences, Symposia, Training programs and Workshops which help to update the latest technologies. Our faculty members and students are engaged in publishing Papers, filing Patents and undertaking Research and Consultancy projects.

About the Conference

The primary objective of the conference is to provide an interactive platform for academicians, professionals, industry experts, practitioners, policy makers and research scholars from different domains to engage in discussion based on social issues related to ever-growing and challenging economic environment. It will also act as a discussion forum to get acquainted with latest developments and trends in the field of engineering and technology coupled with their implications and developments for the organizations.

Call for Papers

  • Prospective authors are encouraged to submit their original, unpublished research paper (which is not under review in any other conference/Journal).
  • All submissions must be in English only.
  • Submissions must be plagiarism free. All submissions will strictly go through a plagiarism check, only plagiarism free submissions will be double blind reviewed.
  • Authors are encouraged to use the IEEE format for manuscript submission. Authors are requested to send the papers to the mail ID iciecs@rmkcet.ac.in
